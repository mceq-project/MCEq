This program is a toolkit to compute the evolution of particle densities that evolve
as a cascade in the Earthâ€™s atmosphere or other target media.
Particles are represented by average densities on discrete energy bins. 
The results are differential energy spectra or total particle numbers.
Various models/parameterizations for particle interactions and atmospheric density profiles are packaged with the code.
