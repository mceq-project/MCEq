Multi-RHS ETD2RK across all backends. ``MCEqRun.solve_multirhs(phi0_matrix,
dtype=…)`` propagates K independent initial conditions through one shared
atmosphere path; ``MCEqRun.solve_fullsky(zenith_grid, azimuth_grid,
dtype=…, bucket_count=…)`` propagates one initial spectrum through every
pixel of a sky grid with bucketed per-pixel atmosphere paths. Both
methods are wired for ``kernel_config`` ∈ ``{numpy_etd2, accelerate_etd2,
mkl_etd2, cuda_etd2}``; the SpMM kernels for MKL (``mkl_sparse_d_mm`` /
``_s_mm``) and CUDA (eager ``cupyx.scipy.sparse @ dense_2d``) are new on
this branch and ship in fp32 + fp64. The fused ETD2 post-apply C kernels
move out of the Mac-only ``MCEq.spacc`` extension into a new
platform-neutral ``MCEq.etd2_kernels`` extension built on all platforms.
On SATORI (RTX 3090) at SIBYLL21 dim 7986, ``cuda_etd2`` holds flat at
~0.024 ms/iter/RHS fp32 through K=256 (~1300× per-RHS over Mac
Accelerate fp64); full-sky 5×5 bucketed multipath is 97 ms/pixel fp32
(9.3× over the numpy reference).
